Design simple SCT supervisors for a robot.

- Controllable events: move_forward, move_backward, random_walk, clockwise_turn, counterclockwise_turn, full_rotate
- Uncontrollable events: obstacle_front, obstacle_left, obstacle_right, path_clear

Include all uncontrollables (obstacle_front, obstacle_left, obstacle_right, path_clear).  
Make transitions deterministic and safe.  
Do not enable move_forward if obstacle_front is active.  
Keep 2–4 states.
Use only given events

- Move random walk to cover the area while avoiding obstacles.
