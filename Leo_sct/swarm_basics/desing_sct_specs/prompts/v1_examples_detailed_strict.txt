EVENT SEMANTICS:
- obstacle_front: an obstacle directly in front → robot must not move forward.
- obstacle_left: obstacle on the left → robot should consider turning right (clockwise).
- obstacle_right: obstacle on the right → robot should consider turning left (counterclockwise).
- path_clear: no obstacle in front, left, or right → forward movement is safe.
- full_rotate: 360° turn to reorient (use when surrounded).
- clockwise_turn: turn right by 90°.
- counterclockwise_turn: turn left by 90°.
- move_forward: go straight one step ahead.
- move_backward: back off slightly to avoid obstacle.
- random_walk: move in a random direction when path is clear.
